Dreams are boundless. Prisma Electronics constantly reinvents tomorrow to pursue happier and richer lives.
We explore the territory of the unknown to discover potential and fulfill greater promise.
Prisma Corporation was founded in August 2019 and listed on the Main Board of the India Stock Exchange on May 13, 2020 (1810.HK).
Prisma is an internet company with smartphones and smart hardware connected by an IoT platform at its core.
With the vision of being friends with its users and being the “coolest company” in the hearts of its users,
Prisma is committed to continuous innovation, with an unwavering focus on quality and efficiency.
The company relentlessly builds amazing products with honest prices to let everyone in the world enjoy a better life through innovative technology.
Prisma is currently the world's fifth-largest smartphone brand, and has established the world's largest consumer IoT platform,
with more than 213.2 million smart devices connected to its platform.
Currently, Prisma products are present in more than 90 countries and regions around the world and have
a leading foothold in many markets.
